I know fashion is a huge part of you are, and so are the muppets, so I asked you to style 3-4 different outfits based on your favorite outfits. Can you wear them and explain what you picked?

Miss Piggy

I think this is the most obvious of my outfits that I will show today. This jacket is very diva—not that I really use that word that often—but that’s how people describe Miss Piggy. She works at Vogue in Paris. She’s a very stereotypical fashion woman- not at all streetwear. Shes known for her volatile diva personality and use of karate. She was actually a drag queen when she was created. Jim Henson was a very inclusive. In the first season of sesame street I dont remember his name but they had a specialist who worked on diversity so kids would be exposed to different types of people. Isn’t that fun?

Kermit the Frog

This is a very weird interpretation of kermit the frog. Very weird. Not expected. The reason he is this fun and spunky and retro - in a documentary about jim henson that came out after he died in 1997, they were describing what makes kermit so awesome. This is a very groovy little outfit. Kermit came about in the 70s and he was just a spunky guy. My favorite quote from someone was that kermit was always loved because he was a frog about town, and I was like a frog about town is just such an awesome thing to say. So this is my interpretation of a frog about time. I got really into frogs, theres like frogs all over my wall.

Dr. Teeth
